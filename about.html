About us

Creating a space where technology becomes accessible, practical, and inspiring.

Okene Tech Hub is built to empower young people with the skills, support, and confidence they need to grow in a digital world. Our vision blends education, innovation, and belonging.

Mission

Empower the next generation of innovators.

We exist to equip young people with digital skills, access to community, and pathways into technology through practical training, workshops, and collaboration.

Vision

Build a leading force for technology growth in Kogi State and beyond.

We want Okene Tech Hub to be known as a place where ambition is welcomed, potential is nurtured, and new ideas become real opportunities.

Our values

Everything starts with people.

These principles shape the way the hub teaches, collaborates, and shows up for its members.

Accessibility

Learning should feel welcoming, inclusive, and reachable for people at different starting points.

Practicality

Training should connect clearly to real skills, real projects, and real confidence.

Community

Growth happens faster when people can learn, share, build, and encourage one another.

Innovation

We want members to move beyond passive learning and begin thinking like creators and problem solvers.

Opportunity

The hub is designed to connect talent with mentorship, visibility, and pathways to bigger possibilities.

Ready to belong?

Be part of a tech community that believes in your potential.

Whether you want to learn, mentor, partner, or support the mission, there is a place for you here.
